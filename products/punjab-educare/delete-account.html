Delete Account Request

Punjab Educare Teacher Account Deletion

Important Information
  • Only teacher accounts can be deleted (students don't have accounts)
  • Processing time: Up to 4 weeks after email verification
  • Educational content: Submitted questions that were approved will remain in our database
  • Irreversible: Account deletion cannot be undone

Choose Your Preferred Method

Method 1: Web Form

Fill out the form below for a quick and secure deletion request.

This must be the same email you used to register your teacher account.
Method 2: Direct Email

Send a deletion request email directly to our accounts team:

Email: accounts@punjabeducare.co.in

Important: Make sure to send the email from your registered email address for verification.

What Happens Next?
  1. We verify your identity using your registered email address
  2. You'll receive a confirmation email acknowledging your request
  3. Your account and personal data will be deleted within 4 weeks
  4. You'll receive a final confirmation when deletion is complete
