How It Works

1. Export Chat from WhatsApp

In WhatsApp, go to a chat, tap "More Options" > "More" > "Export chat" and select "Include media".

2. Select Zip in App

Open WhatsZip Viewer, tap the "+" button, and select the exported .zip file from your device.

3. View Your Chat

Your chat history, including all media, will be displayed instantly within the app.
