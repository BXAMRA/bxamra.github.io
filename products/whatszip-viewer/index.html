WhatsZip Viewer ​

Effortlessly view your exported WhatsApp chats including photos, videos, and audio — all offline and privately.

Key Features ​

  • Complete Privacy: All your chat data is processed locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any servers.
  • Full Media Support: View attached photos, play videos, and listen to audio messages directly within the app.
  • Interactive Links: Clickable links within your chat messages open directly in your external browser.
  • Easy Navigation: Scroll through your entire chat history with a familiar interface.
  • Mobile Friendly: Designed for seamless experience on all mobile devices.
  • System Messages Display: Clearly distinguish system messages (e.g., group creations, members added/removed) from regular chats.

How It Works ​

  1. Export Chat from WhatsApp: In WhatsApp, go to a chat, tap “More Options” > “More” > “Export chat” and select “Include media”.
  2. Select Zip in App: Open WhatsZip Viewer, tap the “+” button, and select the exported .zip file from your device.
  3. View Your Chat: Your chat history, including all media, will be displayed instantly within the app.
