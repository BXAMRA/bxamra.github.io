# Updating from v1

If you are currently using the older version of **Punjab Educare Teacher** (the one you installed in 2025), this guide will help you remove it and install the new version correctly.

The new app is faster, lighter, and updates itself automatically — so you will never need to do this again.

::: warning Before you begin
The old and new versions of the app **cannot run at the same time**. You must fully remove the old version first before installing the new one.
:::

---

## Step 1 — Open Control Panel

1. Press the **Windows key** on your keyboard.
2. Type `Control Panel` and press **Enter**.
3. The Control Panel window will open.

## Step 2 — Go to Programs

1. Inside Control Panel, look for **"Programs"** or **"Programs and Features"**.
2. Click on **"Uninstall a program"** (it is listed below "Programs").
3. A list of all apps installed on your computer will appear. Wait a moment for it to fully load.

## Step 3 — Find the Old App

1. Scroll through the list and look for **"Punjab Educare Teacher"**.
2. Click on it once to select it (it will be highlighted in blue).

::: tip Can't find it?
Try clicking the **Name** column header to sort the list alphabetically — this makes it easier to find apps starting with "P".
:::

## Step 4 — Uninstall

1. Once **"Punjab Educare Teacher"** is selected, look at the toolbar at the top of the list.
2. Click the **"Uninstall"** button that appears.
3. A confirmation window may appear asking _"Are you sure you want to uninstall this program?"_ — click **Yes**.
4. Wait for the uninstall to complete. This usually takes less than a minute.
5. The app will disappear from the list when it is done.

## Step 5 — Download the New App

1. Go to the [Teacher Desktop](/punjab-educare/teacher-desktop) page.
2. Click the **Download for Windows** button.
3. A file named `educare-teacher_windows-x86_64-setup.exe` will be saved to your **Downloads** folder.

## Step 6 — Install the New App

1. Open your **Downloads** folder and double-click the downloaded file.
2. A setup wizard will open — click **Next** and follow the on-screen steps.
3. If a **Windows SmartScreen** warning appears saying _"Windows protected your PC"_:
   - Click **"More info"**
   - Then click **"Run anyway"**

   This is normal for new apps — the app is safe to install.

4. Once installation is complete, the app will open automatically.

## Step 7 — Log In

1. Enter your registered **email address** and **password**.
2. You will be taken to your dashboard just like before.

::: info Your data is safe
Your question submissions and account information are stored on the server — nothing is lost when switching versions.
:::

## Done ✓

You are now running the latest version of **Punjab Educare Teacher**.

From this point on, the app will **check for updates automatically** every time it starts. When an update is available, an **"Update and Restart"** button will appear in the title bar — just click it and you're done.

You will never need to manually update again.
