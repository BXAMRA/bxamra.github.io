# Question Submissions

Punjab Educare supports teacher-contributed questions through a guided submission workflow with internal review before publication to the main question bank.

## Where submissions happen

Question submission is available across both mobile and desktop apps. The workflow and rules are consistent to keep review and publishing predictable.

## Workflow

1. Review requirements and confirm eligibility to submit.
2. Select academic context (class, subject, topic, and language where applicable).
3. Enter questions using a structured editor designed for consistent formatting and review.
4. Submit for internal review.
5. Publication to the main bank occurs only after approval by the trusted internal admin team member.

## Requirements & rules

**Minimum submission size**

- A minimum of 10 questions is required per submission.

**Supported formats**

- Multiple choice
- True/False

**Consistency rule**

- For multiple choice questions, the correct answer is placed in option 1 and 4 options are required.

## Review and quality control

- Every submission is reviewed before publication.
- This review step is used to maintain quality, correctness, and curriculum alignment across classes and subjects.

## Submission restrictions

Teachers may be temporarily restricted from submitting new questions based on prior submission outcomes (for example, repeated rejections). When restricted, submissions are blocked until eligibility is restored.

## Consent and retention

By submitting a question set, teachers confirm:

- The provided information and questions are correct to the best of their knowledge.
- They consent to permanent publication and unrestricted use of the submitted questions (as described in the [Intellectual Property & Collaboration Notice](/punjab-educare/intellectual-property-collaboration-notice)).
- Submitted questions cannot be deleted after submission, including after an account deletion request.

::: info Why consent exists
The question bank is designed as a curated, long-lived resource for learners. The submission workflow (review → approval → publication) is structured to support quality control, long-term reuse, and continuity of the bank over time.
:::
